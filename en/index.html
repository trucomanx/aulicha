Aulicha: Chronicles in the Andes

In this work, the reader will learn about a series of stories that took place in the Andes Mountains of Peru. They show some of the experiences of my father, Aurelio (1956), when he was just a child. Through his words and his unique perspective, we will be able to enter into the lives and small adventures of the mountain dwellers; thus learning about their problems, their joys and the lessons that life provides them.
